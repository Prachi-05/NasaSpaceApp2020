The Pale Blue Dot

Thirty years ago the furthest man-made object from the Earth ,Voyager 1,swiveled around and peeked back at the Solar system through its digital eyes. The image it sent back changed our perpsective of the Earth forever;There lay our home,Earth, like a small speck of dust suspended on a sun beam. It showed us how small and insignificant compared to the vast cosmic background we were. But it united humanity like never before. Perhaps no one better than Carl Sagan could have put "The Pale Blue dot" into perpsective.
